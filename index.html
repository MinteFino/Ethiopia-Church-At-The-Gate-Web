About Us

We’re a vibrant Ethiopian church family committed to the teachings of Christ and serving our local and global community. Join us for worship, fellowship, and opportunities to grow in faith.

  • Christ-centered teaching
  • Welcoming community
  • Outreach & mission

Meet Our Pastor

Pastor Mathewos Koyrra has been serving our church for over 10+ years. With a deep passion for community and faith, he guides our congregation with wisdom and compassion.

His vision is to build a welcoming church where everyone feels loved and empowered to grow spiritually.

His Ministry Focus

  • Spiritual growth and discipleship
  • Youth and family outreach programs
  • Community service and engagement
  • Preaching with clarity and relevance
“Our church is a family where everyone has a place. Together, we seek to serve God by loving one another and impacting our community with hope and faith.”
